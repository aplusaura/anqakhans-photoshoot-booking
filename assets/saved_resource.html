This Photoshoot Booking Agreement (“Agreement”) is entered into between AnQa Khans, an Egyptian short-term hospitality brand (“Host”), and the brand or individual identified below (“Client”). By signing this Agreement, the Client acknowledges having read, understood, and agreed to all terms and conditions herein.

Booking Details

Article 1 — Access & Scheduling

  1. 1.1Check-in time is 12:00 PM. The Client acknowledges that housekeeping may still be in process upon arrival.
  2. 1.2Any early morning access prior to 12:00 PM is subject to additional fees and must be agreed upon no later than the evening before, subject to availability.
  3. 1.3Check-out must be completed by 11:00 PM on the booking date.
  4. 1.4Extended check-out (overnight until 11:00 AM the following day) is only permitted with prior written approval from AnQa Khans. The full names and count of all guests staying overnight must be confirmed in advance.
  5. 1.5A confirmation message from the AnQa Khans Guest Relations team will be sent prior to the shoot to verify all details and collect the Client’s Estimated Time of Arrival (ETA). Failure to respond may result in cancellation of access.

Article 2 — Team & Personnel

  1. 2.1The Client agrees not to exceed the team size confirmed and stated in this Agreement. Any additions to the team must be requested and approved by AnQa Khans in advance.
  2. 2.2The Client is responsible for the conduct of all crew, talent, and guests present during the shoot, and for their compliance with this Agreement.
  3. 2.3AnQa Khans may refuse entry to, or remove, any person exceeding the approved team size or behaving inappropriately on the property.

Article 3 — Content & Property Rules

  1. 3.1Revealing or inappropriate clothing/content is strictly prohibited, including (without limitation): lingerie or underwear, bikinis or swimwear, sheer or transparent outfits, deep cleavage or exposed chest, excessively short skirts or shorts, and provocative poses or suggestive content.
  2. 3.2AnQa Khans reserves the right to pause or terminate any shoot that does not align with community standards or platform policies, without refund of fees paid.
  3. 3.3Skincare content is strictly limited to the face, arms, and hands. Filming of any other body part for skincare purposes is not permitted.
  4. 3.4The Client shall treat the property with care. Any damage, staining, or excessive mess may result in deductions from the Security Deposit.
  5. 3.5All props, equipment, packaging, and personal items must be removed from the property by the end of the booking.
  6. 3.6The Client shall respect the building and neighbours. Loud music, disturbances, or disruptive behaviour are not permitted.
  7. 3.7Filming in outdoor areas or shared/common areas must be requested and approved by AnQa Khans in advance.

Article 4 — Payment, Deposit & Cancellation

  1. 4.1The booking is confirmed only once the Rental Fee and/or required deposit, and this signed Agreement, have been received by AnQa Khans.
  2. 4.2The Client shall pay a refundable Security Deposit, which serves as cover for any damage, loss, or breach of this Agreement, in lieu of a separate insurance policy. The deposit is refunded within 5 business days of check-out, net of any deductions.
  3. 4.3Cancellations made 14+ days before the booking date: 50% of the deposit is refundable. Cancellations made less than 7 days before, or no-shows: non-refundable.

Article 5 — Liability, Ownership & Governing Law

  1. 5.1The Client shall indemnify and hold AnQa Khans harmless against claims, damages, or liabilities arising from breach of this Agreement, property damage, personal injury, or content produced during the shoot, save for AnQa Khans’ gross negligence.
  2. 5.2All photographs and footage produced during the shoot belong to the Client. AnQa Khans may request a limited selection of behind-the-scenes content for its own portfolio, with credit to the Client.
  3. 5.3This Agreement is governed by the laws of the Arab Republic of Egypt, with disputes subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the competent courts of Cairo.
  4. 5.4This Agreement may be validly executed by electronic signature under Egyptian Law No. 15 of 2004 on Electronic Signatures, carrying the same legal effect as a handwritten signature.
